## How to Order

### Step 1: Choose Your Products
Browse our product list above and decide what you'd like to order.

### Step 2: Send Us Your Order
Message us on WhatsApp with:
- Products and quantities (e.g., "Almond London x2, Sambal Bilis x3")
- Preferred pickup/delivery date and time
- Delivery address (if delivery)
- Any special notes

### Step 3: Payment
After we confirm your order, make payment via:
- Online Banking (Maybank / CIMB)
- Touch 'n Go eWallet
- GrabPay

### Step 4: Receive Your Order
- **Pickup:** Collect from our location in Cheras
- **Delivery:** We'll deliver to your doorstep!

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long can the cookies last?**
A: Our cookies can last up to 3 weeks if stored in airtight containers at room temperature. Refrigeration can extend to 6 weeks.

**Q: Are your products halal?**
A: Yes, all our products are 100% halal. We use only halal-certified ingredients.

**Q: Can I customize gift boxes?**
A: Yes! Let us know your preferences and budget, and we'll create a custom hamper for you.

**Q: Do you offer bulk/wholesale pricing?**
A: Yes, for orders of 20 units or more, please contact us for special pricing.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with my order?**
A: Customer satisfaction is our priority. Contact us within 24 hours of receiving your order if there are any issues.

**Q: Can I cancel or modify my order?**
A: Yes, you can cancel or modify up to 24 hours before the scheduled delivery. Orders already in preparation cannot be cancelled.

**Q: Do you ship nationwide?**
A: Currently we only deliver within Klang Valley. For Peninsular Malaysia delivery, please contact us for courier options.
