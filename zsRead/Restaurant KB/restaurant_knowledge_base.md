## Reservations / Booking

### How to Book
- Message us on WhatsApp to make a reservation
- Provide: Date, Time, Number of People (Pax)
- We will confirm your booking within 30 minutes

### Booking Policy
- Maximum booking: 20 people per table
- For groups larger than 20, please call us directly
- Please arrive within 15 minutes of your booking time
- Bookings held for 15 minutes only

### Cancellation
- Free cancellation up to 2 hours before booking time
- For same-day cancellations, please notify us as soon as possible

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do you have vegetarian options?**
A: Yes! We offer vegetarian versions of Nasi Goreng, Mee Goreng, and various vegetable dishes. Please inform us when ordering.

**Q: Is the food halal?**
A: Yes, all our food is 100% halal certified.

**Q: Do you accept credit cards?**
A: Yes, we accept Visa, Mastercard, and all major e-wallets (Touch 'n Go, GrabPay, Boost).

**Q: Is parking available?**
A: Yes, free parking is available in front of our restaurant.

**Q: Can I modify my booking?**
A: Yes, you can modify your booking up to 2 hours before the reservation time.

**Q: Do you have a kids menu?**
A: We don't have a specific kids menu, but we can prepare smaller portions of any dish upon request.
