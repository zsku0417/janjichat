# Janji Chat - AI-Powered WhatsApp Chatbot Platform

## What is Janji Chat?

Janji Chat is an AI-powered WhatsApp chatbot platform that helps businesses automate their customer service. It uses advanced AI technology (GPT-4o-mini) to understand customer messages and provide intelligent, context-aware responses 24/7.
