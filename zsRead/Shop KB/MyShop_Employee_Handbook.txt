3. WORK HOURS & ATTENDANCE
================================================================================

STANDARD OPERATING HOURS:
Monday to Saturday: 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Sunday: Closed (except during festive seasons)

SHIFT SCHEDULES:
- Morning Shift: 8:00 AM - 4:00 PM (includes 1-hour lunch break)
- Afternoon Shift: 11:00 AM - 7:00 PM (includes 1-hour lunch break)

ATTENDANCE POLICY:
- Arrive at least 10 minutes before your shift starts
- Clock in/out using the provided attendance system
- Notify supervisor at least 2 hours before shift if unable to attend
- Three unexcused absences may result in disciplinary action

OVERTIME:
- Overtime must be pre-approved by management
- Overtime rate: 1.5x regular hourly rate (weekdays)
- Overtime rate: 2x regular hourly rate (weekends/public holidays)

4. CODE OF CONDUCT
================================================================================

PROFESSIONAL BEHAVIOR:
- Treat all colleagues, customers, and vendors with respect
- Maintain a positive and cooperative attitude
- Dress appropriately (clean, modest attire; hair net in production area)
- No smoking on premises
- No consumption of alcohol during work hours
- Mobile phone use limited to break times (except for work purposes)

- Keep all business information confidential
- Do not share recipes, supplier details, or customer data
- Do not discuss company matters on social media without approval

CONFLICT OF INTEREST:
- Do not engage in activities that compete with MyShop
- Report any potential conflicts to management
- Do not accept gifts from suppliers exceeding RM50 in value

5. CUSTOMER SERVICE STANDARDS
================================================================================

OUR SERVICE PROMISE:
Every customer interaction should leave a positive impression.

RESPONSE TIME STANDARDS:
- WhatsApp messages: Reply within 30 minutes during business hours
- Order confirmations: Within 1 hour of payment verification
- Complaints/Issues: Acknowledge within 2 hours, resolve within 24 hours

COMMUNICATION GUIDELINES:
- Use polite and professional language
- Address customers by name when possible
- Be patient and understanding, even with difficult customers
- Always thank customers for their business

SAMPLE RESPONSES:

Greeting:
"Hi [Name]! Thank you for contacting MyShop. How may I assist you today?"

Order Confirmation:
"Thank you for your order! We've received your payment and will prepare
your items for [delivery/pickup] on [date]. Order reference: [#]"

Handling Complaints:
"We sincerely apologize for the inconvenience. Let me look into this
immediately and get back to you with a solution."

LANGUAGES:
- Respond in the language the customer uses
- Supported languages: English, Bahasa Malaysia, Chinese (Mandarin)

6. PRODUCT HANDLING GUIDELINES
================================================================================

FOOD SAFETY PRIORITIES:
Food safety is non-negotiable. Follow these guidelines strictly.

PERSONAL HYGIENE:
- Wash hands before handling food (minimum 20 seconds)
- Wear clean uniforms, aprons, and hair nets
- Do not work if you have symptoms of illness
- Keep fingernails short and clean; no nail polish
- Remove jewelry before entering production area

STORAGE GUIDELINES:

Cookies & Biscuits:
- Store in airtight containers at room temperature (25°C or below)
- Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture
- First-In-First-Out (FIFO) rotation

Sambal & Sauces:
- Refrigerate at 4°C after opening
- Check for signs of spoilage before packing
- Ensure lids are properly sealed

Packaging Standards:
- Use food-grade packaging materials only
- Include production date and best-before date
- Check for damaged packaging before dispatch

7. HEALTH & SAFETY
================================================================================

WORKPLACE SAFETY:
- Report any hazards immediately to supervisor
- Keep work areas clean and organized
- Follow proper lifting techniques (bend knees, not back)
- Know the location of first aid kit and fire extinguisher

EMERGENCY PROCEDURES:

Fire:
1. Alert others by shouting "FIRE!"
2. Activate fire alarm if available
3. Use fire extinguisher if safe to do so
4. Evacuate via nearest exit
5. Assemble at designated meeting point (front parking area)
6. Do not re-enter building until cleared

Medical Emergency:
1. Call for help immediately
2. Administer first aid if trained
3. Call emergency services: 999
4. Stay with the person until help arrives

ACCIDENT REPORTING:
- Report all accidents to supervisor immediately
- Complete incident report form within 24 hours
- Seek medical attention if needed
